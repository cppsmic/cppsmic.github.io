About SMIC


The Club is an on-campus organization facilitated by students open to all CPP students. Upper-class students that have taken the Applied Portfolio Management course serve in senior leadership positions while other students serve in supporting and learning roles.

Our Values


MISSION: Our campus focuses on a polytechnic education that enforces a “Learn by Doing” academic philosophy. The university serves as a center of excellence for our diverse students and faculty from a variety of backgrounds including many first-generation college students. In addition to learning, we strive to sustain a culture of inclusivity, community engagement, and societal responsibility. The SMIF program seeks to provide equal opportunities to all students. Specifically, the university offers CFA SMIF, CFA Institute Research Challenge, Dr. J. Douglas Ramsey Bloomberg Lab, and Argus competition for students to prepare them for future career success, regardless of their backgrounds.


VISION: CPP SMIF’s goal is to maximize risk-adjusted returns for our clients while keeping their needs and expectations at the core of our investment decisions. Our investment decisions are driven by fundamental research with the goal of building long-term wealth for our clients. The portfolio construction and management processes are positively impacted by the selection of a diverse team to provide multiple perspectives. We believe markets are efficient in the long-term; however, short-term inefficiencies do occur. By utilizing an active management approach, we can capitalize on those opportunities to add additional value for our client. Our approach will balance risk and reward with a conservative inclination. Overall, the Student Managed Investment Fund will embody Cal Poly Pomona’s “Learn by Doing” philosophy.
